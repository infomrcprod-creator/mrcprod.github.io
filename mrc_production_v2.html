    Process

    How It Works

    Step 01

    Request & Brief

    Fill in the request form with your reference tracks, genre, BPM, and project details. The clearer the brief, the sharper the result.

    Step 02

    Quote & Terms

    You receive a detailed quote within 24–48 hours, covering deliverables, revision rounds, and rights transfer conditions. No surprises.

    Step 03

    Production

    Work starts after deposit. You receive milestone previews — idea sketch, full arrangement, pre-master — at key stages for feedback and direction.

    Step 04

    Delivery

    Final files delivered on Google Drive: mastered WAV, unmastered mix, stems, and MIDI. Full rights transfer on final payment.
